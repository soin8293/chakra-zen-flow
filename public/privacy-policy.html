Privacy Policy for ZenFlow

Last updated: August 21, 2025

1. Information We Collect

ZenFlow is designed to protect your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our meditation and chakra healing services:

  • Local storage data for your meditation progress and preferences
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience
  • Bookmarked articles and meditation sessions (stored locally)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized meditation experiences
  • Save your progress and preferences
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

Your meditation data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit personal meditation data to external servers. Any analytics data is anonymized and used solely for app improvement.

4. Third-Party Services

ZenFlow may use anonymous analytics services to understand app usage patterns. No personal or meditation data is shared with third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your local data by uninstalling the app
  • Disable analytics through app settings
  • Request information about data collection practices

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@zenflow.app

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.
